ESIC Operations

Standard Operating Procedures (SOP)

Internal Manual for Employees & HR Personnel — Employees' State Insurance Corporation

1. ESIC Card (e-Pehchan) Download Process

Follow these steps to safely access and download your digitized ESIC identity card:

A. Self-Download via Member Portal

  1. Go to the official website: esic.gov.in.
  2. Navigate to the Insured Person/Beneficiary Login section.
  3. Enter your unique 10-digit Insurance Number (IP Number) as the User ID and submit your password. Complete the Captcha challenge.
  4. On the main dashboard, look for the link labeled "View/Download e-Pehchan Card".
  5. Verify your profile data summary on screen, then click Download PDF.

B. Alternate Download via UMANG App

  1. Open the UMANG Mobile App on your smartphone.
  2. Search for ESIC Department Services.
  3. Select "Download e-Pehchan Card", verify via the OTP sent to your registered mobile, and save the document.
Pro-Tip: Once printed on a plain sheet of paper, get it counter-signed and stamped by your employer's HR group to ensure instant approval during hospital admissions.

2. Addition of Dependent Family Members

To extend medical privileges to immediate family members, additions must be filed digitally through the employer dashboard:

Action Steps:

  1. The employee must provide clear copies of the dependent's **Aadhaar Card** and proof of dependency (such as a birth certificate for children or income declarations for parents) to the HR Desk.
  2. The HR Administrator will log into the ESIC Employer Portal.
  3. Go to Employee Profile Management → Register/Modify Employee Details.
  4. Input the employee’s IP Number and open the "Family Details" tab.
  5. Click on "Add Family Member", fill in the exact name, date of birth, relationship status, and upload the Aadhaar copy as primary proof.
  6. Submit the data changes. The portal updates immediately, modifying the structural layout of your e-Pehchan card dynamically.

3. Addition of Nominee in ESIC

Nominations ensure smooth processing of cash benefits/gratuity processing under statutory claims:

Action Steps:

  1. The employer accesses the backend profile of the Insured Person (IP) using the Employer Code.
  2. Select the "Update Nominee Details" segment from the interactive menu list.
  3. Fill in the fields accurately: Full Legal Name, Relationship matrix (e.g., Spouse, Mother), current Residential Address, and dynamic share distribution percentage (totaling 100%).
  4. Save information and generate the declaration print-out sheet. Have the employee verify and execute a physical signature for corporate records.

4. Process Note for IP Particular Correction

Guidelines for correcting critical operational identity parameters such as Name, Date of Birth (DoB), Gender, or Father's Name:

Correction Workflow:

  1. Initiation: The employee submits an offline correction request accompanied by an Aadhaar Card or official Government ID proofs.
  2. Online Submission: The Employer accesses the "Modify Employee Details" gateway tool, selects fields requiring fixes, inputs correct data, and uploads the scanned legal credentials.
  3. Approval Processing Window:
    • Minor corrections align automatically within the network dashboard.
    • Critical corrections (e.g., a Date of Birth variance exceeding 3 years) route automatically to the local **ESIC Branch Office field queue** for review. Officials review and resolve submissions within a target window of 3 business days.
Warning Note: Ensure personal parameters mirror the text strings exactly as formatted on the Aadhaar Card database to bypass strict automated validations.

5. Process for Updation of Address & Contact Details

Ensures systemic delivery updates and keeps your local dispensary maps accurately localized to your place of living:

Action Steps:

  1. Supply updated demographic documents (Aadhaar or Rent agreements) and your active working phone number to the corporate processing department.
  2. The HR representative loads the online account profile via the Employer Login portal.
  3. Select the specific tabs: "Update Residential/Permanent Address" and "Change Mobile Details".
  4. Save the entries to trigger updates across the server grid. A verification confirmation code is dispatched to the updated mobile endpoint tracking index.
