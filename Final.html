Downloading Your e-Pehchan Card

Step 01

Open the ESIC Employee Portal. Once loaded, click on the Insured Person option.

Step 02

The web page will safely redirect you to the designated Employee Login Portal interface.

Step 03

Login with your credentials using your ESIC IP Number.
*If you forget your password, select 'Forget Password' to receive an OTP on your registered mobile number, generate a new password, and log in.

Step 04

After a successful login, you will be presented with the main User Dashboards.

Step 05

Locate and select the Download e-Pehchan Card option.

Step 06

Click on VIEW OR E-PECHAN CARD to safely pull up and download your document in PDF format.

How to Add Dependents

Step 01 - 03

Log in to the ESIC Employee Portal under the Insured Person interface using your IP Number.

Step 04

Once inside your dashboard tracking area, choose the Update Particulars menu action item.

Step 05

On the alternative employee options dashboard panel, select Family Details.

Step 06

You will arrive at the family management tracking interface. Click on the Edit link to add or tweak dependent listings and map their verification assets.

Step 07

Fill in the essential data fields precisely:

  • Full Name (Matching your Aadhaar records explicitly)
  • Date of Birth & Gender selection
  • Relationship classifications (Spouse, Son, Daughter, Father, etc.)

Pick your validation certificate criteria from the drop-down menu, upload the scanned copy (Max 200KB), and click on the Add button.

A unique reference confirmation number will prompt successfully, moving the workflow status to 'Pending for Approval'.

Enrolling Nominee Details

Steps 01 - 03

Access the ESIC Portal, switch to the Insured Person channel, and complete the validation screen inputs with your password.

Step 04 - 05

Navigate directly to Update Particulars and click on the NOMINEE DETAILS card option.

Step 06

The nomination profile window will launch. Supply the required fields and submit your supporting documentation attachments.

📋 Mandated Nomination Certificates Checklist:

Addition of Mother

Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, and an authorized Income Certificate.

Addition of Father

Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, and a Notarized Income Certificate copy.

Addition of Spouse

Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, and your official Marriage Certificate copy.

Addition of Children

Under 2 Years: Birth Certificate or Aadhaar.
Over 2 Years: Aadhaar Card is mandatory.

Bank Account Tracking Updates

Government ID validation copy alongside matching Voter ID, PAN, or Aadhaar Cards.

Updating Core Profile Information

Steps 01 - 03

Log in to the official ESIC Portal using your primary account IP Number credentials.

Step 04 - 05

Go to Update Particulars and select the PERSONAL DETAILS link box configuration.

Step 06 - 07

Correct any outdated parameters in the form. Pick your document validation category type and attach the evidence file from your device directory.
*Supported Formats: PDF, JPG, JPEG | Maximum file size limit: 200KB.

Step 08

Click the Upload button, acknowledge your legal criteria consent by selecting the Declaration Checkbox, and click Update to submit your changes for approval.

📋 Acceptable Evidence Framework for Profile Adjustments:

Post-Marriage Name Change

Aadhaar Card accompanied by an official Marriage Certificate.

Clerical / Typo Corrections

Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, or Voter Identification Card options.

Legal Name / Gender Shifts

A legally certified and notarized Affidavit document.

Date of Birth Adjustments

10th Standard Marksheet, Birth Certificate, or School Transfer Certificate (TC).

Spouse Verification (Male Account)

Wife’s Aadhaar Card (listing husband's name) OR Marriage Certificate.

Spouse Verification (Female Account)

Personal Aadhaar Card (listing husband's name) OR Marriage Certificate.
