How To Download ESIC Card?

Step 1

Login to the ESIC member portal. Select the Insured Person option.

Step 2

Type your 10 Digit ESIC IP Number and password to log in.

Note: If you forget your password, click 'Forgot Password'. It will send an OTP to your registered mobile number. Enter the OTP and make a new password.

Step 3

Once you will successfully login to your ESIC portal user dashboard will reflect.

Step 4

Click on the button that says View/Print e-Pehchan card .

Step 5

New Window will get open, where all companies in which your were ESIC member will reflect Select PhonPe - VIEW/Download E-PECHAN CARD option. Your card will get download in PDF file.

How to add dependent in ESIC?

Step 1

Login to the ESIC member portal. Select the Insured Person option.

Step 2

Type your 10 Digit ESIC IP Number and password to log in.

Note: If you forget your password, click 'Forgot Password'. It will send an OTP to your registered mobile number. Enter the OTP and make a new password.

Step 3

Once you will successfully login to your ESIC portal user dashboard will reflect.

Step 4

After a successful login, click on the option named Update Particulars.

Step 5

On the next screen, click on Family Details Option.

Step 6

Now you will see a screen to add or edit family members. Click on the Edit link to start adding your family dependent's information.

Step 7

Type the member's details: Full Name (Exactly as written on their Aadhaar card), Date of Birth, Gender, and Relationship (like Spouse, Son, Daughter, Father etc).

Step 8

Select the Type of Document Proof from the dropdown box. Click on the Choose File button, select the document saved on your device (Must be less than 200KB PDF, JPG & JPEG file size), and click open.

Step 9

Click on the Add button. A reference number will be generated on your screen. Your request is now sent and is pending for approval with Employer and ESIC Branch office.

Note: Share your submitted request details with your employer on esic@phonepe.com

How to add Nominee in ESIC?

Step 1

Login to the ESIC member portal. Select the Insured Person option.

Step 2

Type your 10 Digit ESIC IP Number and password to log in.

Note: If you forget your password, click 'Forgot Password'. It will send an OTP to your registered mobile number. Enter the OTP and make a new password.

Step 3

Once you will successfully login to your ESIC portal user dashboard will reflect.

Step 4

After a successful login, click on the option named Update Particulars.

Step 5

From the list, click on the option named NOMINEE DETAILS.

Step 6

Fill in the nominee details as per their Aadhaar card, select the proof type, attach your file, and submit for verification.

Note: Share your submitted request details with your employer on esic@phonepe.com

How to update personal and bank details in ESIC?

Step 1

Open the website. Click here to visit ESIC Employee Portal. Select the Insured Person option.

Step 2

The website changes to the Employee Login page setup.

Step 3

Type your ESIC IP Number and password to sign into your account.

Step 4

Go to the main dashboard menu links and click on Update Particulars.

Step 5

From the options list, choose and open PERSONAL DETAILS (or select Bank Details depending on what you want to change).

Step 6

The screen will let you edit your information. Type the new correct data into the empty boxes.

Step 7

Select your Proof Type from the dropdown list. Click on the Choose File button to upload your document copy.
*Rules: Files must be PDF, JPG, or JPEG format only, and under 200KB file size limit.

Step 8

Click on the Upload button. Mark the little Declaration Checkbox to give your consent, then hit the final Update button to submit your change request.

📋 Document Requirements for Personal & Bank Corrections

Correction Type Requested Aadhaar Card / Ration Card PAN Card / Voter ID Marriage Certificate 10th Marksheet / Birth TC
Name Change (Due to Marriage)
Minor Spelling Fixes (Typo error)
Gender Change
Date of Birth (DOB) Fix
Bank Account Update (Govt / Ration)

Aadhaar Seeding via Mobile App

Step 1

Download the ESIC Health Connect Application and the Aadhaar Face RD Application on your mobile phone from the app store.

Step 2

Open the ESIC app and tap on the Insured Person option.

Step 3

Enter your 10 Digit ESIC IP number into the box.

Step 4

Type the security OTP number that you received on your registered mobile phone.

Step 5

Once the ESIC Health Connect app logs in successfully, tap and open the Service option menu.

Step 6

Tap on the Aadhaar seeding option, then select the seed aadhaar button link.

Step 7

Enter your 12 Digit Identity card number into the form and tap on the Face Authentication option button.

Once your Aadhaar seeding completed it will Aadhaar status verified.
