How To Download ESIC Card?

Step 1

Open the website. Click here to visit ESIC Employee Portal. Select the Insured Person option.

Step 2

The website will now open the Employee Login page.

Step 3

Type your ESIC IP Number and password to log in.

Note: If you forget your password, click 'Forgot Password'. It will send an OTP to your phone. Enter the OTP and make a new password.

Step 4

After logging in, you will see your main profile Dashboard page.

Step 5

Click on the button that says Download e-Pehchan Card.

Step 6

Click on VIEW OR E-PECHAN CARD. Your card will download on your screen as a PDF file.

How to add dependent in ESIC?

Step 1

Open the website. Click here to visit ESIC Employee Portal. Select the Insured Person option.

Step 2

The website will open the Employee Login page.

Step 3

Type your ESIC IP Number and password to log in.

Step 4

After a successful login, click on the option named Update Particulars.

Step 5

On the next screen, click on Family Details.

Step 6

Now you will see a screen to add or edit family members. Click on the Edit link to start adding your family dependent's info.

Step 7

Type the member's details: Full Name (Exactly as written on their Identity document), Date of Birth, Gender, and Relationship (like Spouse, Son, Daughter, Father).

Step 8

Select the type of Document Proof from the dropdown box. Click on the Choose File button, select the document saved on your device (Must be less than 200KB size), and click open.

Step 9

Click on the Add button. A reference number will be generated on your screen. Your request is now sent and is pending for approval.

How to add Nominee in ESIC?

Step 1

Open the website. Click here to visit ESIC Employee Portal. Select the Insured Person option.

Step 2

The website will redirect you to the Employee Login page.

Step 3

Type your ESIC IP Number and password to log in.

Step 4

After logging in, look at the dashboard options and select Update Particulars.

Step 5

From the list, click on the option named NOMINEE DETAILS.

Step 6

Fill in the nominee details on the screen, select the proof type, attach your file, and submit for verification.

📋 Document Requirements Checklist

Nominee / Query Type Identity Document PAN Card Income / Notary Certificate Marriage Certificate Birth Certificate
Mother Addition (Standard)
Father Addition (Notarized)
Spouse Addition
Child (Age below 2 years)
Child (Age above 2 years)
Bank Updates / Correction (Govt ID/Voter)

How to update personal details in ESIC?

Step 1

Open the website. Click here to visit ESIC Employee Portal. Select the Insured Person option.

Step 2

The website changes to the Employee Login page setup.

Step 3

Type your ESIC IP Number and password to sign into your account.

Step 4

Go to the main dashboard menu links and click on Update Particulars.

Step 5

From the options list, choose and open PERSONAL DETAILS.

Step 6

The screen will let you edit your information. Type the new correct data into the empty boxes.

Step 7

Select your Proof Type from the dropdown list. Click on the Choose File button to upload your document copy.
*Rules: Files must be PDF, JPG, or JPEG format only, and under 200KB file size limit.

Step 8

Click on the Upload button. Mark the little Declaration Checkbox to give your consent, then hit the final Update button to submit your change request.

📋 Document Requirements for Personal Corrections

Correction Type Requested Identity Document Marriage Certificate Notarized Affidavit 10th Marksheet / Birth TC
Name Change (Due to Marriage)
Minor Spelling Fixes (Typo error) (PAN/Voter)
Legal Name / Gender Change
Date of Birth (DOB) Fix

Aadhaar Seeding via Mobile App

Step 1

Download the ESIC Health Connect Application and the Aadhaar Face RD Application on your mobile phone from the app store.

Step 2

Open the ESIC app and tap on the Insured Person option.

Step 3

Enter your 10 Digit ESIC IP number into the box.

Step 4

Type the security OTP number that you received on your registered mobile phone.

Step 5

Once the ESIC Health Connect app logs in successfully, tap and open the Service option menu.

Step 6

Tap on the Aadhaar seeding option, then select the seed aadhaar button link.

Step 7

Enter your 12 Digit Identity card number into the form and tap on the Face Authentication option button.

Once the process is finished, your dashboard profile will display your status as 'Verified'.
