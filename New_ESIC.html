How To Download ESIC Card?

Step 1

Login to the ESIC Employee Portal. Once you open the portal, select the Insured Person option.

Step 2

After selecting Insured Person, the web page will redirect you to the Employee Login Portal.

Step 3

Login with your ESIC IP Number.

*If you forgot your password: Select 'Forgot Password'. It will send an OTP to your registered mobile number. Enter the OTP to generate a new password and log in.

Step 4

After log-in is successful, you will see the main Dashboard interfaces.

Step 5

Select the option Download e-Pehchan Card.

Step 6

Select VIEW OR E-PECHAN CARD and safely download your generated PDF file.

How to add dependent in ESIC?

Step 1

Login to the ESIC Employee Portal. Once open, select the Insured Person option.

Step 2

The webpage redirects automatically to the Employee Login Portal window.

Step 3

Log in using your unique ESIC IP Number. (Utilize 'Forgot Password' OTP recovery if needed).

Step 4

Once inside the dashboard, locate and select the Update Particulars menu option.

Step 5

From the Employee optional Dashboard, choose Family Details.

Step 6

The family tracking screen will load. Here, you can add/edit family dependents and map proofs. Click on the Edit link to adjust details or begin adding.

Step 7

Fill out the complete required information fields:

  • Full Name (Exactly as written on Member's Identity document)
  • Date of Birth & Gender
  • Relationship classification (Spouse, Son, Daughter, Father, etc.)

Choose the validation proof type from the dropdown, click Choose File to attach the document from your system (Max size allowed: 200KB), and click Add.

Result: A unique tracking reference number will generate successfully and the workflow shifts to 'Pending Approval'.

How to add Nominee in ESIC?

Step 1 - 3

Access the ESIC Portal, choose Insured Person, and securely log in using your IP Number credentials.

Step 4

Navigate directly to the Update Particulars interface link.

Step 5

On your configuration dashboard, choose NOMINEE DETAILS.

Step 6

The system opens your updating tab area. Input required identity details and prepare your evidence files according to the rules below.

📋 Required Documents for Nominee Addition:

  • For Mother: ID Proof, PAN Card, and an Income Certificate.
  • For Father: ID Proof, PAN Card, and a Notarized Income Certificate.
  • For Spouse: ID Proof, PAN Card, and an official Marriage Certificate.
  • For Children (Under 2 years old): Official Birth Certificate or ID Proof.
  • For Children (Over 2 years old): ID Card.
  • Bank Account Updates: Government ID Proof, Voter ID, PAN, or matching ID Card documents.

How to update personal details in ESIC?

Step 1 - 3

Open the portal, pick Insured Person, log in with your credentials, or perform a password reset if locked out.

Step 4 - 5

Select Update Particulars from your primary portal options, then click on PERSONAL DETAILS.

Step 6

The page displays edit boxes for your personal attributes. Update any information tracking error in the relevant mandatory sections.

Step 7

Select your proof category. Attach files matching formatting specifications (Allowed: PDF, JPG, JPEG | Maximum size limit: 200KB).

Step 8

Click Upload. Carefully mark the consent/declaration checkbox fields, then click Update to submit your official correction request.

📋 Accepted Proofs for Specific Corrections:

  • Name Change via Marriage (Females): ID Proof, Marriage Certificate.
  • Minor Corrections (Spelling/Clerical Errors): ID Card, PAN Card, or Voter ID.
  • Legal Name / Gender Amendments: Official Legally Notarized Affidavit.
  • Date of Birth Corrections: 10th Standard Marksheet, Registered Birth Certificate, or School Transfer Certificate (TC).
  • Spouse Linkage validation (Male Employee): Wife's ID Card showing husband's name, OR a Marriage Certificate.
  • Spouse Linkage validation (Female Employee): Her own ID Card showing husband's name, OR a Marriage Certificate.
  • Photo Updates: Clean Passport size photo clear image file.
